Summary

I am a Staff Applied Scientist at Afresh, a company which aims to reduce food waste by helping grocery stores optimize their inventory management decisions. Previously, I was a Senior Research Scientist at Amazon. I earned my Ph.D. in Operations Research at UC Berkeley, where I was advised by Professor Ilan Adler. I hold an undergraduate degree in Mathematics from MIT. I am primarily interested in discrete optimization problems, especially in settings that include graphs and machine learning.
